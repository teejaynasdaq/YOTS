About Us

YOTS Ministries International is an organization established by young ministers of the Word, dedicated to prayer and teaching other youth about God's teachings.

We bring the gospel to you

Matthew 28:18-20 “All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me. Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything that I have commanded you.”, God has instructed us to go forth and make disciples.

Ephesians 6:15 "And with your feet fitted with the readiness that comes from the gospel of peace.""

What We Believe In

What we believe in as the body of Christ

The Goodnews

God calls us to be fishers of men—our movement's mission is to reclaim the youth by truthfully spreading the Lord's gospel.

Leadership

YOTS strives to empower young people, equipping them to become strong and effective leaders within ministry.

Prayer

The Word of God urges us to pray continuously, and YOTS firmly upholds the transformative power of prayer.

The Gifts Of The Spirit

YOTS firmly believes in the truth of everything written in the Word of God, including the spiritual gifts bestowed by Him.

Giving

The Bible teaches that the hand that gives is greater than the one that receives, and YOTS embraces giving as one of its core principles.

Evangelism

YOTS was founded with the mission of leading people to the body of Christ, focusing on empowering young individuals to actively go out and win souls for the Kingdom of God.
